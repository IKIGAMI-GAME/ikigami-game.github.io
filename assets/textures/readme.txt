These texture files should be downloaded from a texture source:

earth_daymap.jpg - Main Earth texture (land + ocean)
earth_nightmap.jpg - Earth at night with city lights
earth_clouds.png - Cloud layer texture (with transparency)
earth_normal.jpg - Normal map for terrain elevation
earth_specular.jpg - Specular map for water reflection

You can find high-quality free Earth textures on sites like:
- NASA Visible Earth (https://visibleearth.nasa.gov/)
- Solar System Scope (https://www.solarsystemscope.com/textures/)
- Celestia Motherlode (http://www.celestiamotherlode.net/)
